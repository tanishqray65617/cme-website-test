About JNV Selection Test

The JNVST is a non-verbal, figure-based exam designed to ensure that talented children from rural areas can compete without language barriers. The focus is heavily on Mental Ability.

Eligibility Criteria

Class Age Residence Education
Class VI 10 to 12 Years Same District Passed Class V

Arithmetic

25% Weightage

Language
