About the Test

The CME Talent Hunt is designed to recognize and support meritorious students. It is not just an exam, but an opportunity to assess your standing at a national level and win financial aid for your defence coaching journey.

Scholarships are applicable on Tuition Fees ONLY. Residential and mess charges are payable in full and are not covered under this scheme.

How to Get a Scholarship?

We offer financial aid through multiple categories.

Talent Hunt Performance

Score high in our Scholarship Test to win fee waivers ranging from 10% to 100% based on your rank.

Academic Excellence

Direct scholarship for School Toppers or students securing 90%+ grades in their previous academic session.

Defence Background

A flat 20% Fee Concession is provided to wards of Army, Navy, and Air Force personnel as a token of respect.

Achiever's Benefit

Existing students who qualify for at least one Sainik School written exam get a 10% fee deduction for the next year.
